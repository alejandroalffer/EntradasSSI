Privacy & conditions

Convergence Wallet by IN2 is an application that allows you to store credentials of different assigned issuers to the Alastria network.

Credentials are stored on the device in JWT format, being presented when required to verify your data.

The request and presentation of credentials follows the Alastria Id protocol, which complies with the regulation of eIDAS and the good practices of the e-Identity Workshop Report, of the EUBlockchain Forum and Observatory.

This protocol allows the sharing of cryptographically signed data and its validation in a decentralized manner, allowing its validation quickly and safely.

By installing this application, you accept that the data and credentials obtained are stored on your device until it is uninstalled.
